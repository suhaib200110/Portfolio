About Me

Electrical Engineer (Electronics) • Machine Learning Engineer • Embedded Systems Developer

Who I Am

As an Electrical Engineer with a strong foundation in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Embedded Systems, I specialize in developing innovative solutions for real-world problems across Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and microcontroller-based systems.

My portfolio includes projects such as vehicle and number plate classification, stock market prediction, YOLO-based smart parking management systems, and embedded electronics projects involving sensors, control systems, and IoT-based monitoring.

In addition to ML expertise, I have hands-on experience working with Arm Cortex-M microcontrollers, enabling me to design efficient and optimized embedded applications involving real-time processing, sensor integration, and hardware-level programming.

Proficient in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, embedded C/C++, and advanced data analytics, I combine software intelligence with hardware understanding to build robust, impactful, and scalable engineering solutions.
